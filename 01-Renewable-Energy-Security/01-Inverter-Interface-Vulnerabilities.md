# Inverter Interface Vulnerabilities & Mitigation

**Status:** Draft / Outline

## The Core Premise
[cite_start]Modern renewable energy systems, such as solar panel arrays and smart inverters, are heavily networked. While this allows for remote monitoring and efficiency tracking, it introduces severe cyber-physical vulnerabilities. A compromised inverter can lead to localized power disruption or act as a pivot point into a broader corporate network.

## Key Focus Areas:
1. [cite_start]**Default Credential Exploitation:** Analyzing the risks of default vendor credentials left active on management interfaces (e.g., Huawei inverter deployment protocols).
2. **Firmware Integrity:** The risks of over-the-air (OTA) updates for smart inverters and how to verify firmware signatures before deployment.
3. **Network Isolation:** Strategies for segmenting telemetry and power management data from the primary business network to prevent lateral movement.
